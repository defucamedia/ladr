Welcome to LADR

LADR is an award winning, multi-faceted consulting firm that specializes in landscape architecture, sustainable design, and greenway master planning. Since 1994, LADR has provided consulting services to not-for-profit agencies, private and corporate clients, as well as all levels of government. LADR is routinely involved in a variety of creative and sustainable design initiatives including:


  • low impact development (LID)
  • eco-roof design and implementation
  • on-site storm water management
  • preparation of environmental protection covenants
  • school grounds greening
  • urban park development
  • urban greenway master planning
